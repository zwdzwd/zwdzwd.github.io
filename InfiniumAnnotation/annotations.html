Probe_ID Knowledgebase

(1) Probe_ID: Probe ID
(2) Knowledgebase: Knowledgebase label:
  • ProbeType: Infinium-I or -II
  • ChromHMM: Generated using 833 ENCODE chromHMM calls from ENCODE ver.2, PMID: 22955616
  • REMCChromHMM: ChromHMM consensus with at least 607 samples
  • Blacklist: Based on ENCFF356LFX (2020-05-05), PMID: 31249361
  • CTCFbind: CTCF binding sites Wang et al. 2012 Genome Research, PMID 22955990
  • ImprintingDMR: Imprinting DMRs based on Court et al. 2014 Genome Research, PMID 24402520
  • rmsk1: RepeatMasker Lv1
  • rmsk2: RepeatMasker Lv1
  • CGI: CpG island shores (up to 2kb between CpG islands), CpG island shelves (regions 2 to 4kb between CpG islands) and open seas (rest of the genome)
  • H3K9me3HighConfidence: Based on CpGs having H3K9me3 marks
  • Tetranuc2: Tertranucleotide context of all CpGs based on tetranucleotide: SCGS,WCGW,SCGW, N (unknown bases) are excluded
  • ...
