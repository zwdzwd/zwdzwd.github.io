(1-3) CpG_chrm, CpG_beg, CpG_end: the location of the target. CpG_beg is 0-based coordinate and CpG_end is 1-based. The coordinates should have a span of 2 nucleotides for CpG probes, or 1 nucleotide for CpH and SNP probes.
(4) probeID: Probe ID
(5) TFBS_location: Imprinting control region in the format: chr:beg-end
(6) TFBS_summit: Imprinting control region in the format: chr:beg-end
(7) sample: Imprinting control region in the format: chr:beg-end
(8) TF: Imprinting control region in the format: chr:beg-end
(9) loc_summit: mat/pat
